Easily Summarize Video Content with AI

SumIt is a versatile tool designed to streamline the process of summarizing video content using artificial intelligence. By harnessing AI capabilities, SumIt offers users a convenient solution for extracting key insights and highlights from videos efficiently. With its intuitive interface and automated summarization feature, SumIt empowers users to quickly grasp the essence of video content, enhancing productivity and knowledge retention.

How to use it?

Choose a video file and press "Summarize", it's that easy!

Supported video formats: mp4, avi, wav, mov.

Maximum file size: 25mb.
